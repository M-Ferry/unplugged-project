Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged Retreat?

From the airport, you will dispatched to our halfway point where you will take donkeys onlong the base of the Redfeather Monutain, taking in the exquisite beauty of the Colorado River. From there you will be hoisted individually past the Crazydogs caverns up to Standing Bear Cliff (its only a 150 drop) finally you'll take the 3.5 miles down the snakelike Wildwoman road to our retreat.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged Retreat?

If you have people willing to find their hidden abilities and push them to their limits, you belong at our retreat.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Choose and check in to a variety of accommodations for intimate getaways or large gatherings of up to 500 guests, located on 240 acres of Utah ranchland. Then fill every moment with adventure, discovery, or nothing at all. Our accommodations come along with delightful surprises for a wonderful experience.

Is there really no wifi available?

This digital detox offers you an opportunity to disconnect from your devices and connect with nature, your team and your combined abilities.
